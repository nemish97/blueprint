    About Us

    "Blue Print" is a Mumbai based interior contracting firm that has been providing interior services. We are a young firm with a team of professionals who share a passion for the best & are constantly working to provide best services to our clients. Our complete execution team can transform your requirements to finely crafted end to end solutions keeping the time line and budget in mind. We have successfully executed several prestigious projects serving a wide range of clientele to their full satisfaction. We offer one point solution to complete interior finishing projects.

    Our Team Head Mr.Rakesh Mehta has 30+ years of vast and hands on experience in this field. Before incorporation of organization he was focused and provided services to Architects, Turnkey contracting organizations & Individuals. Understanding the needs of growing industry and having a vision of delivering the finest product directly to stake holders conceptualized this organization. Organization is backed and run by group of dynamic, young and highly calibre professionals capable of handling various job, with a dedicated team of skilled & unskilled workers.

    Our Mission

    In order to fulfill the requirements of clients, we have always offered best Interior Designing Services. Exceptional performance and working beyond expectations has helped us attain trust. This has helped us in building long term relationships with our clients and business partners.

    Our Strength

    A team of supervisors and project managers always work towards accomplishing the targets. We are succeeding at achieving our targets because of such a committed force and have delivered the best everytime.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to expand our reach to stakeholders and individuals, beyond turnkey contracting organisations. As you execute everything according to the standards, you could trust us for 'Quality and Time'.
